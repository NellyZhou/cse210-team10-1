Check how good your home's location is.





Finding a new place to live? Perhaps a crucial factor would be the location. Everyone has different standards of what a good location is — you may want somewhere that is easy to drive from to your everyday work, or you may want somewhere that is more walkable, bikable, or has better access to public transportation. Or maybe you don't care much about commute, and you perfer a place that has better access to resturants or groceries. Our website helps you get personalized scores of your potential new home based on your location preferences.
